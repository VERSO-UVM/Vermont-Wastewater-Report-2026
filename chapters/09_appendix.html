C. Glossary of Terms#

BOD (Biochemical Oxygen Demand): A measure of the amount of oxygen required by bacteria to break down organic matter in water.

NPDES (National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System): A permit program that regulates point source discharges to waters of the United States.

Phosphorus §: A nutrient that can cause excessive algae growth when present in high concentrations in water bodies.

TMDL (Total Maximum Daily Load): A calculation of the maximum amount of a pollutant that a water body can receive while still meeting water quality standards.

TSS (Total Suspended Solids): The amount of solid particles suspended in water.

WWTF (Wastewater Treatment Facility): A facility designed to treat domestic and/or industrial wastewater.
